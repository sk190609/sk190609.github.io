Touch-A-Truck Coming to Pen Argyl!

https://penargylborough.com/

If you're looking for a fun way to start off the month, then we might have the perfect event for you! This event will make for a memorable day for you and your family with plenty of activities. This is the first year Pen Argyl is hosting this event, so come take part in this fun-filled day. Bring your family along for a thrilling day filled with trucks and a variety of entertainment. Join many of your neighbors who are excited for the festival.

Information

The Touch-A-Truck event will take place on Saturday, June 1st, 2024 from 11am to 4pm. The event will be held in the baseball feild of Weona Park. Prices are as low as $10 a child and adults are FREE! Joining us for this occasion will be the Pen Argyl Fire Department, the Northampton PENN DOT, Waste Management, and others! Individuals will be able to enjoy wonderful carousel rides while at the event, and will be able to enjoy edge-of-your-seat miniture golf games. While you're there, don't forget to fill up on food while browsing the vendors. There will also be a live music preformance for entertainment.

A Special Thanks

We hope to see all of you at this exciting family event on Saturday! We would also like to give a special thanks to Weona Park Pool Pals for organizing this event. They have expressed Thank you for all of your support. This event will benefit them in their goals to add a new water feature to Weona Park. We want to give many thanks to our sponsors, Slate Belt Rising, Waste Management, and T. Mac Builders, LLC for making this possible.
